Why I love this movie

Blade Runner 2049 is known for its incredible visuals and futuristic atmosphere. The film uses stunning cinematography, lighting, and color to create a world that feels both beautiful and mysterious. Every scene looks carefully designed, which is one of the reasons the movie stands out and is remembered by so many fans.

This movie means so much to me because I saw it in the theater with my Dad after coming home from a mission. It was my favorite movie when I was trying to be a film major.
